Iberian-Mountain Sporthorses


Our horses all have some Rocky Mountain Horse blood (usually 25%-37.5%). Rockies are an American breed that is famous for being particularly sound in mind and body, and famously curious and interested in bonding with humans. They also have a reputation for being bold and courageous without being hot, and for freezing rather than bolting when spooking. Many of our mares are Iberian-Mountain, a cross between PRE/Lusitano (Iberian) horses and Rocky Mountain. This an increasingly popular cross, and an excellent one due to the breeds' complimentary characteristics and shared heritage.
