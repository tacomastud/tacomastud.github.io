Welcome to Tacoma Stud, based in East Sussex, UK. Our breeding program produces sport horses with a particular focus on temperament, robustness, as well as correctness and athletic ability.

Our foals

Our foals have the very best start in life and we seek the very best competition and/or family homes for them, the goal being that our foal will be your 'horse of a lifetime'.

Purchasing a foal

Our youngsters always sell at weaning, and often have waitlists or are sometimes reserved leading up to foaling. Please get in touch if you would like to express interest in one of our foals!
